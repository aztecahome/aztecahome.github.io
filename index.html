About the Owner

Meet Frankie Ruiz, the proud owner of Azteca Home Services. Frankie opened the business in 2012 with the goal of providing exceptional pest, pool, and landscaping services, all while spending more quality time with his family. He is the proud father of three children, Xavier, Diego, and Savannah, and loves nothing more than spending time with them. When he's not working, Frankie enjoys riding his motorcycle or enjoying a cold beer with his friends and family.

Frankie's dedication to his community runs deep, and it's evident in his commitment to giving back. He is a veteran and takes great pride in his service to his country. Additionally, Frankie is actively involved in community efforts such as food drives, blood donations, and volunteer work. He truly appreciates the opportunities his business provides, and he is dedicated to serving both his clients and his community.
